The Mission

When we hear Speech & Debate, we think of a three word phrase.

And that is: Being. Wholly. Yourself.

We understand the fear of speaking in public, but it doesn’t have to be that way. That’s why we’ve created free courses designed to guide you through the fundamentals of Speech & Debate. Whether you’re looking to step out of your comfort zone or elevate your existing skills, our programs are here to support you.

For seasoned competitors, our bootcamps, led by the world’s top performers in the field, offer unparalleled expertise and insights.
